### Dashboard
- Statistic data view
    - total accounts grouped by status (available, limit-hit, etc.)
    - Upcoming available accounts (1 week or so)
    - Recent limit-hit accounts

### Account List Features
- Search by email and name
- Filter by Account Status and Last used date 

**Notes: ListTile swipe actions (startToEnd: available, endToStart: limit-hit)**

- Last used date (read-only and update when account status changed)
- Next available date
- Account Delete button with AlertDialog
